GlycoTrack

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 10, 2026

GlycoTrack does not create accounts, does not share your data with advertisers, and does not sell your information. Your food log lives on your device.

What GlycoTrack collects

GlycoTrack stores the following data on your device only, in Apple's Core Data framework:

  • Food descriptions you speak or type
  • Timestamps of logged meals
  • Computed GL (Glycemic Load) and CL (Cholesterol Load) values
  • App settings (daily GL budget, physics preferences)

This data never leaves your device to GlycoTrack's servers. There are no GlycoTrack servers.

Voice and speech recognition

When you tap the microphone button:

  • Your voice is transcribed on-device by Apple's Speech framework (SFSpeechRecognizer). Audio is never stored by GlycoTrack.
  • The resulting text transcript (not audio) is sent to the Anthropic Claude API to identify food items and resolve timestamps. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how that text is handled on their end.

Apple's Speech framework may transmit audio to Apple's servers for recognition when on-device recognition is unavailable. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Third-party services

GlycoTrack uses one external API:

  • Anthropic Claude API — receives the text of your voice transcript to parse food names and timestamps. No audio, no personal identifiers, no account information is sent. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

The nutritional reference databases (Sydney GI Database, USDA FoodData Central) are bundled inside the app and do not make network requests.

Permissions

  • Microphone — required to record your voice for meal logging. Only active while you are recording.
  • Speech Recognition — required to transcribe speech to text on-device. No audio leaves your device unless Apple's server-side fallback is triggered (see above).
  • Notifications — optional. Used for a daily reminder to log your meals. No notification content is uploaded anywhere.

You can revoke any permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.

Data retention and deletion

All food log data is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you delete the app. There is no remote backup.

You can soft-delete individual entries from within the app. To wipe all data immediately, delete and reinstall GlycoTrack.

Children's privacy

GlycoTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date at the top will reflect that. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
